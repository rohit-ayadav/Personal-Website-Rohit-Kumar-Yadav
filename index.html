About Me

Greetings! My name is Rohit Kumar Yadav, and I'm a curious and passionate individual from Tarya Sujan in Kushinagar, Uttar Pradesh. In my free time, you can find me exploring the world of the internet, learning new things and staying up-to-date on the latest trends and technologies. I'm also an avid reader of Hindi literature, which has inspired me to develop my own writing skills. When I'm not in front of a screen or buried in a book, I enjoy spending time with my pets, a cow and its calf. They bring me a sense of peace and joy that I can't find anywhere else. In the future, I aspire to become a software developer and make a positive impact in the world of technology.

I am currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Goel Institute of Technology and Management in Lucknow.

As I continue on my educational journey, I am excited to see where my passion for technology and my drive to learn and grow will take me. I am always eager to take on new challenges and push myself to be the best I can be.

Thank you for stopping by my page, and I hope you enjoy learning more about me!

My Education

Education is a crucial part of personal growth and development, and my academic journey has been one of challenges, opportunities, and personal growth. My educational journey began at an early age, where I learned the importance of education and its potential to transform lives.

I completed my high school education at St. Xavier High School in Sameda, Azamgarh, where I graduated with an impressive 87.6%. During my high school years, I studied five subjects which is

  1. Mathematics Standard
  2. Hindi-A
  3. Social studies
  4. English Lit. and Grammer
  5. Science
    6.
including an additional subjects Computer Applications.

After high school, I pursued intermediate studies at Government Jubilee Inter College in Gorakhpur. My experiences at Government Jubilee Inter College were instrumental in shaping my academic and personal growth, allowing me to explore myself and the world around me. However, I also struggled to concentrate on my studies during this time, which impacted my academic performance.

Despite these challenges, I passed my intermediate from Government Jubilee Inter College with a solid 67% grade, point average. This accomplishment was a testament to my perseverance and resilience during this challenging period of my life.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) degree in Computer Science and Engineering at the Goel Institute of Technology and Management (GITM) in Lucknow. Studying at GITM has been a challenging yet rewarding experience, allowing me to gain practical knowledge and hands-on experience in the field of computer science.

Looking ahead, I have ambitious plans for my future. I hope to pursue a career in the field of computer science, leveraging the skills and knowledge I have gained through my academic journey thus far. I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead and the potential to make a positive impact in the world.

In conclusion, my academic journey has been a fulfilling and rewarding experience that has taught me the importance of perseverance, resilience, and determination. Despite facing challenges along the way, I have remained committed to my goals and ambitions. I am grateful for the opportunities I have had to learn and grow, and I look forward to the next chapter in my academic journey.
